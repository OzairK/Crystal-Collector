CrystalsCollector

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game: "Targe Score"
  • There are four crystals below.
  • By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your score to the target score.
  • You lose the game if your total score goes above the target score.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
